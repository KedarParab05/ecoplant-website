Plant Care Essentials

Science-backed tips to keep your plants thriving

💧

Watering Wisdom

Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Stick your finger 2 inches into soil — if dry, it's time.

☀️

Light Requirements

North = low light. East/West = medium. South = bright direct light. Match your plant to your window.

🌡️

Temperature & Humidity

Most tropicals thrive at 18–27°C. Boost humidity by grouping plants or using a pebble tray.

🌱

Fertilizing Schedule

Feed plants monthly during spring/summer with balanced liquid fertilizer. Pause in fall and winter.

🪲

Pest Prevention

Inspect leaves weekly. Neem oil spray is an effective organic solution for most common pests.

🪴

Repotting Guide

Repot when roots peek from drainage holes. Go up 1–2 pot sizes in spring for best results.

✂️

Pruning & Shaping

Remove dead leaves promptly. Cut just above a leaf node at 45° for bushier growth.

🧪

Soil Selection

Cacti need sandy mix; tropicals love rich, chunky aroid mix. Match soil to your plant type.
